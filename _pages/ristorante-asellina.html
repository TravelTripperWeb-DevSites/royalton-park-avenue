Ristorante Asellina

RISTORANTE ASELLINA

Named after the owner of an ancient Pompeian tavern operated by a “freewoman,” Asellina encompasses the ultimate in traditional-meets-modern Italian cuisine. With rich materials of wood, stone and polished concrete framing expansive greenery and fireplaces to provide warmth - the Living Room lounge, main dining room and private patio all provide an ideal environment for dining. Asellina even offers half-portions and finger-foods, all crafted with an authentic Italian twist.
