Forever moments

Royalton is an ideal location for intimate weddings and social events. The 1700 square foot Penthouse is a unique and flexible space overlooking Midtown and Times Square,offering a perfect New York experience for rehearsal dinners, small weddings and other special events leading up to your wedding day.

Forty Four, our restaurant and bar, is perfect for a memorable reception - this popular event space features a modern twist on timeless classics in a unique and sophisticated environment only Royalton can offer.

Flawless service is supplied by on-site professional meeting planners and an expert staff, including sumptuous catering, entertainment planning and state-of-the-art audio-visual services.
