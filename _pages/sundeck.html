Sundeck

sundeck

The Sundeck is located at the top of Gansevoort Park's tri-level rooftop and can hold up to 180 people with flexible seating. Great for soaking up some Vitamin D during the day and sipping a cool cocktail during the evening hours, the Sundeck serves up traditional and frozen drinks, like our classic Frosé cocktail, an extended beer menu, Gansevoort branded fresh coconuts from CoCo & Co as well as delicious café bites.

FROSÉ FRIDAY
Join us on the Sundeck on Friday's from 5PM - 7PM for $15 Frosé cocktails.
