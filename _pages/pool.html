Rooftop Pool at Royalton Park Avenue

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock eat_drink_rooftop_content, type:text %}

The Pool at Royalton Rooftop is located on the 20th floor. Our sprawling 1000 square foot pool deck features a 40 foot indoor/outdoor pool and ivy wall. It is a luxury urban oasis in the sky situated right in the center of Manhattan with unbeatable views of the iconic Empire State Building.

Indulge in light bites and seasonal cocktails in between dips in our heated, year-round swimming pool and relax on our plush chaise lounge chairs.

Our lifeguard attended pool is open daily 7AM until 7PM during spring and summer, and 7AM until 6PM during fall and winter. All guests must present their room key for access, children need to be accompanied by an adult, and proper attire is required.

Complimentary towels are provided, please no smoking.
